Chain Editor Page

A Chain has two columns. The first one labelled "PT" contains Pattern numbers which will play in sequence from top to bottom. The second column, "TR", is the transpose value applied to the Pattern on each step of the Chain. The Transpose value can be positive or negative.

A Chain has a maximum of sixteen steps (00 to 0F) but the Chain will end when a blank cell "--" is encountered.

Note: If you have an empty cell on step 00, Pulsar will think that this Chain is empty and will skip this Chain in the Song.
